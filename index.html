Product Overview

Ductile Iron (DI) Pipes & Fittings

Our DI pipes and fittings are designed for high strength, corrosion resistance, and durability. Ideal for water supply, sewerage, and industrial pipelines, they are available in socketed, flanged, and custom joint types. These pipes comply with IS 8329 standards and are suitable for both above-ground and underground installations.

HDPE Pipes

Made from high-density polyethylene (PE-63, PE-80, PE-100), these pipes are resistant to corrosion and chemicals. Perfect for water supply, irrigation, and industrial applications. Manufactured as per IS 4984:2016.

GI Pipes

Galvanized Iron pipes are zinc-coated to resist rust. Suitable for water supply and general plumbing. Available in Class A, B, C (Light, Medium, Heavy) from 50mm to 150mm as per IS 1239 standards.

MS Pipes

Mild Steel pipes are used in large-diameter, high-pressure applications such as road and railway crossings. Durable and impact-resistant, often installed using trenchless HDD techniques.

Valves (Sluice, Air, Kinetic)

Sluice Valves: For flow control (80–600mm, IS 14846).
Kinetic Air Valves: Release air during pressurization/depressurization (40–200mm).
Air Valves: Prevent air locking in pipelines (≥40mm).

PVC Pipes

Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and easy to install. Suitable for water supply, drainage, and irrigation systems. Available in 20mm to 250mm with pressure ratings of 4–12 kg/cm².

OPVC Pipes

Oriented PVC pipes offer higher strength, flexibility, and impact resistance. Ideal for high-pressure water supply in infrastructure projects and drinking water networks.

DWC Pipes

Double Wall Corrugated pipes with smooth inner walls and corrugated exteriors. Used in drainage, cable ducts, and sewerage. Available in 40mm to 1000mm (SN 4, SN 8 classes).
